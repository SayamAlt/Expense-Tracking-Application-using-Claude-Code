Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

1. Data We Collect

Spendly collects personal financial information that you voluntarily enter into the application. This includes expense amounts, categories, descriptions, and dates. We do not collect any data without your knowledge or consent. All data is entered directly by you as you track your expenses.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your financial data is used solely for the purpose of providing expense tracking functionality within the application. We use your entered information to generate summaries, category breakdowns, and spending insights. Your data is never shared with third parties or used for any purpose beyond helping you track and understand your spending patterns.

3. Data Storage

All your expense data is stored securely within the application. We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your financial information from unauthorized access. You are responsible for maintaining the security of your account credentials. We recommend using strong, unique passwords and logging out when using shared devices.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not sell, trade, or transfer your personal financial data to any third parties. We do not integrate with external financial institutions or payment processors. The only third-party services we use are essential hosting and infrastructure providers necessary to operate the application.

5. Your Rights

You have full control over your data. You can view, edit, or delete any expense entry at any time. If you choose to delete your account, all your associated data will be permanently removed from our systems. You may also export your data for your personal records upon request.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time as our practices evolve. Any material changes will be communicated to you through the application. We encourage you to periodically review this page to stay informed about how we protect your financial information.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us through the application. We are committed to addressing any privacy-related inquiries promptly and transparently.
